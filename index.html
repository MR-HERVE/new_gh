the group is made up of three,Mordecai mwinyi who is the lead singer. Wachira Gatama who is the poet. Kenneth Muya who is the guitarist.

They were set book actors,except Mordecai at the Kenya national theatre.The pay was not as good as the opportunity may sound,they were paid sh.300 per performance.This was a tyring experience for they travelled from one county to another,often they would sleep on the road.In 2013 they sang a political song during election period.That is when they started to think about the name of the group and later decided on H_art the band.They signed a deal with penya records and were enrolled in Sauti Sol Academy.
